What is our Return/Refund Policy ?

A Return and Refund Policy is the agreement where we send our customers about your policies regarding returns and refunds before the time of purchase.

It includes the following pieces of information

  1. How many days you have to return a product.

    2. After receiving the product. We recommend you to call our customer support department (Number mentioned on The Parcel) for any Questions regarding the product and its use. Please Note. Technical support will be free of cost. We provide our Customers 5 days’ satisfaction period to test our product and another 5 days to return our product back.
    Customers can apply for product refund if

    1. They don’t like the product
    2. If the product is not useful to them
    3. If they think that they impulsively bought the product
    4. If they believe that product is not what they ordered
    5. Or any other reason

    We provide them 5 days grace period to test our product and then another 5 days period to ship it back and ask for money by emailing us at comcestserviceslimited@gmail.com

  2. How many days you have to return a product.

    3. After receiving the product customers have 10 days 100% Money back warranty to return the product by shipping. For Example, the product was purchase on 1st of March 2021 and customer shipped it back on 10th of march 2021 we still accept the refund. Customer needs to send us picture of shipment which clearly shows the tracking number and date. Picture should be send by emailing us at comcestserviceslimited@gmail.com. Once we receive the product our customer support team will respond our customers by emailing back that the product was received as it is and has no physical damages. Along with that email our company will send the receipt of Refund. ( which takes 5 to 10 business days to show in customers account)

  3. What is the refund Policy for Service.

    4. Please note if our customer has not provided us written authorization for being charged for the same amount mentioned, in written by our customer support team then they are eligible to get a full refund within 6 months’ even if the services are delivered to them.

We will not charge our customer before the services are delivered and they are happy with our services. Once our customers authorize us for charging the amount only then our company will charge the customer.
